Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 25, 2025

SumNoter does not collect, store, or share any personal information from its users.

1. Camera & Media Access

The app uses your device's camera and media library to extract text from images. These are processed temporarily and never stored or shared.

2. No Tracking

SumNoter does not use analytics, cookies, or tracking tools of any kind.

3. Internet Use

An internet connection is required to send images for summarization. Communication is encrypted. No data is saved on servers.

4. Local Storage

Summaries are saved only on your device. You may delete them anytime.
